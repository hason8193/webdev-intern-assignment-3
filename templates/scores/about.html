About G-Scores

System Overview

G-Scores is a comprehensive high school exam results lookup system for the 2024 academic year. This web application allows students to quickly access their exam scores and provides detailed statistical analysis of performance across all subjects.

Score Classification
Excellent 8.0 - 10.0 points

Outstanding performance, demonstrates mastery of subject content.

Good 6.0 - 7.9 points

Good understanding and application of subject knowledge.

Average 4.0 - 5.9 points

Satisfactory performance, meets basic requirements.

Below Average 0.0 - 3.9 points

Needs improvement, below expected performance level.

Group A Subjects

Group A consists of three core science subjects that are often taken together by students pursuing science and engineering programs:

Mathematics

Foundation for all sciences

Physics

Study of matter and energy

Chemistry

Study of substances and reactions

Our system tracks the top performers in Group A subjects and calculates their combined scores to identify the highest achieving students in this category.
